## Session: Office Hours + CEO Review results

### Key decisions
- **Product positioning:** Trust-brand launch. "Never get double-booked again." Not competing on price, competing on reliability. 19 EUR/month.
- **Target user:** Multi-org professionals (advisors, fractional execs, board members) who juggle calendar providers across organizations.
- **Pricing pivot:** Originally "lower price wins," revised to "charge for quality" after cross-model second opinion challenged the premise. Target users bill 200-500+/hr, they don't churn on price.
- **Sequential ship:** Landing page + design system this week (Ship 1), dashboard + trust features next week (Ship 2). Outside voice caught that building dashboard for zero users is wrong sequencing.
- **Conflict detection isolation:** Runs as SEPARATE BullMQ job after sync, never inside sync loop. Outside voice flagged runtime risk of coupling detection to critical sync path.
- **Conflict language:** "Overlaps detected" not "conflicts prevented" because we can detect but not always prevent.
- **Heartbeat polling:** 5 min interval (not 30s). 30s was overkill for this stage.
